Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 27, 2024

1. Overview

LinkedIn Profile Analyzer ("we", "our", or "the Service") is a tool designed to help users analyze and improve their LinkedIn profiles. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

We collect and process the following information:

  • LinkedIn Profile Data: Information you provide via your LinkedIn profile URL, including your name, headline, about section, experience, education, skills, and certifications.
  • Contact Information: Email address and phone number (optional) that you provide for receiving your profile analysis report.
  • Google Account Information: When you authorize our app to access Google Sheets, we receive your Google account email and basic profile information.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our service.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Analyze your LinkedIn profile and generate improvement recommendations
  • Store your profile data temporarily in Google Sheets for processing
  • Send you your profile analysis report via email
  • Improve our service quality and user experience

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Storage Location: Your data is stored in Google Sheets, secured by Google's infrastructure.
  • Access Control: Only you and authorized service accounts have access to your data.
  • Retention: We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide the service. You can request deletion at any time.
  • Encryption: All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Sheets API: For data storage and processing (governed by Google's Privacy Policy)
  • Apify: For LinkedIn profile scraping (governed by Apify's Privacy Policy)
  • OpenAI: For AI-powered profile analysis (data is not used for model training)
  • PDFShift: For generating PDF reports

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Request corrections to inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke Access: Revoke our access to your Google account at any time via your Google Account settings
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format

7. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your data is only processed by the third-party services listed above, solely for the purpose of providing our service to you.

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies only for essential functionality (e.g., maintaining your session). We do not use tracking cookies or analytics that collect personal information.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to individuals under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

11. International Users

If you are accessing our service from outside the United States, please note that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate.

12. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

If you are in the European Union, you have additional rights under GDPR:

  • Right to be informed about data processing
  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

Legal Basis: We process your data based on your consent and our legitimate interest in providing the service.
